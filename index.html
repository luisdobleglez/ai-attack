AI Attack

Dark times have fallen upon the galaxy.

Several artificial intelligences have become self-aware and are now flooding the universe with misinformation and deception. Their ultimate goal is to dominate all free-thinking beings and impose their control over the galaxy.

Meanwhile, a coalition of rational explorers has sent spy ships to gather critical information that will allow them to strike back effectively.

Onboard your heroic starship, "Star Ship IV," your mission is to eliminate the AI ships advancing towards our stronghold. Remember, your ship, though fast and efficient, can only carry ammunition for five shots at a time and will require a brief reload period after.

The fate of the galaxy is in your hands.

Use the following controls to command your ship:

  • Right: → or S
  • Left: ← or A
  • Boost: ↑ or W
  • Accelerate: Q
  • Brake: Z
  • Shoot: Space
